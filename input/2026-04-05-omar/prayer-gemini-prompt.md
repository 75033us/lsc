# Presentation Summary

**Theme:** This presentation outlines a 49-day (7-week) strategic fasting and prayer program designed by Dallas Living Water Bread of Life Church (達拉斯活泉靈糧堂). Structured around the imagery of the Biblical Tabernacle and a progressive spiritual ascent, the program guides participants through three phases: an inward phase of personal cleansing, repentance, and re-anchoring in the cross (Weeks 1–3); a communal phase of life renewal and corporate unity aligned with Jesus (Weeks 4–5); and a kingdom-level phase of intercessory warfare, standing in the gap for the city of Dallas, and partnering with angelic forces to see God's purposes fulfilled (Weeks 6–7). Each week is paired with specific scriptures, prayer directions, and life postures to cultivate a holistic spiritual revival from the inside out.

**Arc:** Introduction of the 7-week blueprint and its three-phase structure → Week-by-week detailed breakdown (Weeks 1–7) covering scriptures, prayer focus, and action steps → Concluding summary matrix (屬靈復興診斷矩陣) that synthesizes all seven weeks into a unified diagnostic table of spiritual weapons, prayer targets, and life postures

**Audience:** Members and intercessors of Dallas Living Water Bread of Life Church (達拉斯活泉靈糧堂), and potentially broader Chinese-speaking Christian communities seeking a structured corporate fasting and prayer guide for spiritual revival

**Language:** Primarily Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) with occasional English headings and labels

**第一週｜靈性的潔淨與對齊**
