# Board Rotation Meeting Minutes / 理事會輪替會議記錄

## Resolutions / 決議

**RESOLVED**, that **Mr. James Chen and Mrs. Christine Chuang** have tendered their resignation from the Board of Directors, effective April 26, 2026, and the Board accepts the resignation with gratitude for their faithful service.

**茲決議**：**陳建明 夫婦** 已提出辭呈，自 2026年4月26日 起卸任理事職務。理事會接受其請辭，並感謝其忠心的服事。

**茲決議**：聘任 **Benjamin NG 夫婦** 為活泉靈糧堂理事，自 2026年4月26日 起任期 2 年（至 2028年4月25日）。

---

**RESOLVED**, that **Mr. Jay Lin and Mrs. Hannah Hsu** are hereby appointed jointly as a Director of Living Spring Church, effective April 26, 2026, for a term of two (2) years, ending April 25, 2028.

**茲決議**：聘任 **林聖潔 夫婦** 為活泉靈糧堂理事，自 2026年4月26日 起任期 2 年（至 2028年4月25日）。

## Vote / 表決

All three resolutions above were adopted by unanimous vote of the Directors present and entitled to vote.

上述三項決議經出席且有表決權之理事一致通過。
