# 02 · Pre-Service Checklist (T-90 → T-0)

**Purpose**: The Sunday-morning retrieve-and-connect routine. From walking in the door to lights-up, this is the only document you really need.
**Audience**: The PA volunteer on duty.
**Last reviewed**: 2026-05-08 — Shine Zhang

> **Print this. Laminate it. Keep it in the booth.**

---

## Hybrid storage model

Our gear lives in three places:

- **Office (locked)** — mixer, laptop, mics, clicker, USB sticks, DJI, booth kit
- **Booth / stage** — speakers, projector, screen, permanent stage cabling
- **Permanent install** — anything mounted (ceiling speakers, projector, in-wall runs)

Pre-service is **retrieve from office → connect to permanent gear → power-on → check**.

---

## T-90 (90 minutes before service) — arrival

- [ ] Unlock the office
- [ ] **Inventory check** — open the `01-equipment-inventory.md` § "Booth kit" list and confirm everything is present. **Anything missing? Stop. Locate it before retrieving anything.**
- [ ] Carry gear to the booth in **two trips** (don't stack — easy to drop):
  - Trip 1: mixer + cable bag
  - Trip 2: laptop + booth kit + mics + DJI

---

## T-75 — connect

> **Order matters.** Build the signal chain mixer-side first, then plug in source ends. Reduces pops and accidental shorts.

- [ ] Place mixer at FOH position
- [ ] Connect mixer → permanent stage cables (XLR sends to speakers / monitor / inputs)
- [ ] Connect laptop to mixer Ch 15/16 (stereo input — laptop audio)
- [ ] Plug Norwii N29 USB dongle into laptop
- [ ] Connect HDMI (laptop → projector)
- [ ] **Cable hygiene**: no foot traffic across cables. Tape or route any walkway crossings. Strain relief at every connector — no sharp 90° bends.
- [ ] **Power last**: only after all signal cables are seated, plug in IEC power for mixer + speakers + amp + laptop.

### Power-on sequence (no speaker pops)

> **Source first, amp last. Reverse on shutdown.**

- [ ] Mixer ON (master fader fully **down**)
- [ ] Laptop ON (Caffeinate / display-stay-awake on)
- [ ] Wireless mic receivers ON
- [ ] **Then** speakers / amplifier ON
- [ ] Bring master fader up to nominal (~unity). Listen for any hum or pop — fix at source, never just turn it down.

---

## T-60 — slides + laptop ready

- [ ] Open PowerPoint, dock-pinned
- [ ] Confirm Google Drive desktop is **synced** (the cloud icon shows the green check)
- [ ] Open `~/Documents/LSC-Service/YYYY/MM-DD/` for today
- [ ] Open `service.pptx` (or merge of worship + sermon decks if both arrived already — see `05-slides-and-laptop.md`)
- [ ] Test display mirroring → projector. Click through 3–5 slides via clicker.
- [ ] **Range test the clicker** — walk to back of the room and try advancing slides. Pulpit-distance test.
- [ ] Replace clicker AAA battery if it shows weak — **do not save batteries on Sunday morning**. Spare goes back in booth kit.
- [ ] macOS Do-Not-Disturb ON. Notifications, AirDrop banners, screen-share prompts all silenced.

---

## T-45 — recording (DJI) prepped

- [ ] DJI battery charged (≥ 80%) — see `07-recording.md` for storage card check
- [ ] Set up DJI at its position (TBD: tripod / shelf location)
- [ ] Test record 30s → playback → confirm audio + video
- [ ] **Don't start recording yet** — that's a T-5 step

---

## T-30 — mic check (sound check happens now if not at rehearsal)

> See `03-rehearsal.md` for the full sound-check sequence. Quick version below.

- [ ] Power-on each wireless mic; check battery LED on receiver
- [ ] Walk each mic to its position; PFL the channel; set TRIM so peaks hit the yellow zone, never red
- [ ] All AUX 1 PRE switches **pressed** (monitor send is fader-independent)
- [ ] Worship-team monitor mix: ask each musician what they need to hear, adjust with AUX 1 knobs (not the fader)
- [ ] Pastor wireless: PFL Ch 11/12; have someone speak at speaking volume from the pulpit; set TRIM. Verify the mono signal sums to L+R as expected (audio is centered, not panned hard L).

---

## T-15 — slide freeze

> **Hard rule**: at T-15, the slide deck is **frozen**. No further edits to the deck (Google Slides or local). Emergencies only.

- [ ] Final merged deck saved as `service.pptx` in today's folder
- [ ] First slide showing (pre-service music background, announcements, or welcome)
- [ ] Pre-service music playing through Ch 15/16 at conversational background level

---

## T-5 — final checks

- [ ] DJI: **press record** now
- [ ] Mixer: master fader at unity
- [ ] Laptop battery? (Should be plugged in; verify charging icon)
- [ ] Pastor mic ON, fader at unity, ready
- [ ] Lights at pre-service level (whoever runs lights)
- [ ] Cup of water at booth (optional but appreciated)

---

## T-0 — service starts

You're done with setup. Now you're running the service — see `04-service-runsheet.md`.

---

## What "good" looks like

- Speakers don't pop when you power them on
- Pastor mic level peaks yellow, never red
- Worship team can hear themselves without complaining
- Clicker advances every slide first try, even from the back
- DJI is recording (red light visible)
- Pre-service music plays for at least 5 min before T-0

If any of these is off → diagnose now, don't hope it fixes itself.
